# Product Notes

Gutty is a free poop analyzer and gut health tracker.

## Product direction

- Make gut tracking funny enough to use and serious enough to be safe
- Help people build a baseline from repeated poop logs
- Turn awkward body data into useful pattern recognition
- Keep private logs local by default
- Let people opt into community suggestions without pretending strangers can diagnose them

## Core promise

Everyone says to trust your gut. Gutty helps you figure out whether your gut has earned that trust today.

## MVP behavior

- Log Bristol type, color, amount, urgency, comfort, hydration, fiber, stress, notes, and red flags
- Score the latest gut pattern from recent logs
- Surface simple wellness observations
- Escalate red flags with conservative language
- Provide a local community wall for poop stories and suggestion requests

## Near-term roadmap

- Add edit and delete controls for logs
- Add CSV export
- Add food and medication tags
- Add trend charts by week
- Add reminders without shame or streak anxiety
- Add private share links or public profiles for people who intentionally want to publish their poop
- Add clinician-friendly PDF summaries

## Design principles

- Funny is welcome; reckless is not
- Red flags should interrupt the joke
- One variable at a time beats chaotic self-experimenting
- Community suggestions should be kind, practical, and non-diagnostic
- People should understand exactly what data is private and what data they publish
